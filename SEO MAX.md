\## SEO Rules to Follow



\### Technical

\- Every page: unique title tag, unique meta description, canonical tag

\- Schema markup on every page — choose the most appropriate type

\- All internal links use descriptive anchor text

\- Image alt text on every image

\- HTML is semantic: article, section, nav, main, header, footer



\### Content

\- Write for humans first, search engines second

\- No keyword stuffing

\- Answer the full question, not just the surface question

\- Longer is better only when the content is genuinely useful

\- Use the exact keyword in: H1, first paragraph, one H2, meta description



\### Link Strategy

\- Every blog post links to at least 2 other blog posts

\- Every blog post links to /faq/

\- /faq/ links to relevant blog posts

\- Everything links back to /
